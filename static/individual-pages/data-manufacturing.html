Data Manufacturing: Creating data with origin

The revolutionary process of manufacturing data objects with immutable origin tracking and ownership binding

Rootz pioneered Data Manufacturing - the revolutionary process of creating data objects with built-in origin tracking, ownership binding, and immutable provenance chains. What is core to our model is that when a wallet is created, genesis data is recorded in the first blocks on the chain. The first genesis block will always record the ownership of the product or data. In addition, the system that created the data is also recorded as a genesis block. This innovation solves the fundamental challenge of data authenticity in the digital age.

The Data Manufacturing Revolution

Traditional data lacks verifiable origin - you cannot prove where it came from, who created it, or whether it has been tampered with. Rootz's Data Manufacturing process creates data objects that carry immutable proof of origin through genesis blocks. From this innovation, the owner can dynamically update any of the information on the chain while maintaining immutable proof of authentic creation:
